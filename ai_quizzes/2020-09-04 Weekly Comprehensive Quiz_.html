    Instructions

    This is a short quiz for the "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence" class. It may include questions concerning any reading, video, or class meeting that has been assigned to date. The quiz contains ten questions, and you will have 30 minutes to answer all of those questions. You will have only one opportunity to complete this quiz, so please do not start it unless you have a 30 minute block of time to complete it. Different sequences of questions may be presented to different students. The quiz is "open book". Credit for the quiz accounts for approximately 2% of your course grade.

    The quiz must be completed by the end of the day on Friday, September 4, 2020.
